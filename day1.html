Day 1 – Kingdom Resort Exploration

Focus

Gentle arrival, orientation, light on‑site fun, secure bookings, conserve energy.

Core Flow (Flexible)

  • Arrival & unwind: check‑in, hydrate, quick setup.
  • Orientation stroll: pool, splash zone, mini golf, play areas, boma, spa, booking desk.
  • Relax block: pool loungers, mini golf round, spa enquiry, light snack.
  • Optional light activity: archery intro, quad bike enquiry, short bike loop, kids splash.
  • Sunset moment: quiet stroll & photos (ask staff for best lookout).
  • Dinner: on‑site, takeaway, or self‑cater.
  • Evening (energy dependent): fire pit & stars; confirm safari / Zip 2000 / spa; optional short Sun City drive.

On‑Site Highlights

  • Relax: Pool deck, Spa
  • Light Fun: Mini Golf, Bike hire loop, Kids splash & play
  • Active / Bookable: Archery, Quad Biking, Safari / Activity Desk
  • Atmosphere: Boma fire area, open lawn (stargazing)

Indicative Costs (pp)

  • Pool & splash areasIncluded
  • Mini Adventure GolfR50–R80
  • Bike Hire (1h)R120–R180
  • Archery SessionR180–R250
  • Quad Biking (short)R400–R650
  • Boma (informal)Free

Purpose

Acclimatize, lock scarce bookings early, keep energy reserves for safari + adventure days.
